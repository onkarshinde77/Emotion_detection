How it works

📋 Instructions

  1. Click "Start Detection" to begin
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Position your face in front of the camera
  4. The system will detect and label emotions in real-time
  5. Click "Stop Detection" to pause

🎯 Detection Features

  • Real-time face detection
  • 7 emotion categories
  • Confidence scores
  • Color-coded labels
  • Multiple face support

💡 Tips

  • Ensure good lighting
  • Face the camera directly
  • Keep a neutral background
  • Stay within 2-3 feet of camera
